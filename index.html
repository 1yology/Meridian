About the practice

Meridian Biostatistics is a specialist research consultancy serving the medical and life-sciences community. We embed alongside clinical teams, residents, and faculty to translate ambitious research questions into rigorous, publishable evidence.

Our work is grounded in three principles: methodological rigour, transparent documentation, and absolute confidentiality. We do not outsource. Every project is led by a senior statistician with peer-review experience.

M

Mission

To raise the methodological standard of clinical research through accessible, principled, and reproducible statistical practice.

V

Vision

A research ecosystem where every published figure is defensible, every method pre-specified, and every dataset honoured by the analysis it deserves.

P

Principles

  • Pre-registered analysis plans
  • Reproducible code, version-controlled
  • Encrypted data handling, NDAs on request
  • Plain-language interpretation alongside output

A measured process.

Predictable, transparent, and documented at every stage.

  1. 01

    Submit Request

    Share your research question, data summary, and timeline through our intake form or a brief email. Confidentiality is presumed; NDAs are issued on request.

    ~ 1 day
  2. 02

    Initial Consultation

    A 30-minute scoping call to align on objectives, candidate methods, deliverables, and a fixed engagement schedule. You receive a written proposal within 24 hours.

    30 min
  3. 03

    Data Review

    Structured data audit: variable inventory, missingness, coding integrity, and assumption diagnostics. You receive a brief data-quality memo before analysis begins.

    2–4 days
  4. 04

    Analysis & Reporting

    Pre-specified analysis is executed in versioned code. You receive publication-grade tables, figures, and a methods paragraph drafted for direct insertion into your manuscript.

    5–14 days
  5. 05

    Revisions & Final Delivery

    Two rounds of revisions are included as standard. Final delivery includes annotated code, a reproducibility README, and a 30-day support window for reviewer responses.

    As needed

07

Frequently asked.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Most Essential engagements are delivered within 5 business days. Standard engagements typically run 10–14 days, and systematic reviews are scoped individually given their iterative nature. Expedited timelines are accommodated where capacity allows.

How is data confidentiality handled?

All data is transferred over encrypted channels and stored on access-controlled, jurisdiction-appropriate infrastructure. Mutual NDAs are issued on request, and identifiable data is processed under explicit data-handling agreements. Our default is to work with de-identified datasets.

Which statistical methods do you support?

The full standard repertoire — descriptive and bivariate inference, generalised linear models, survival analysis (Kaplan-Meier, Cox, competing risks), mixed-effects and longitudinal models, propensity-score methods, meta-analysis with heterogeneity diagnostics, and Bayesian regression. We routinely use R, SPSS, Python, and Stata.

What is your revisions policy?

Essential engagements include one revision round; Standard includes two; Advanced includes unlimited reasonable revisions within the agreed scope. Reviewer-response support is included for 30 days after final delivery on Advanced engagements and is available as an add-on on others.

Do you provide co-authorship?

We follow ICMJE authorship guidelines. When our intellectual contribution to study design and analysis meets ICMJE criteria, co-authorship is appropriate and discussed in advance. Otherwise, we are pleased to be acknowledged in the appropriate section of the manuscript.

Can you support an active manuscript revision?

Yes. We routinely take on reviewer-response engagements, including additional analyses, methodological clarifications, and rebuttal drafting. Share the reviewer comments and current manuscript and we will return a scoped proposal within one business day.
